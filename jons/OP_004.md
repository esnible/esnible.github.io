# Occasional Paper No. 4

BOOKS IN PRINT - EAST ASIAN NUMISMATICS

Following our listing of Arabic books available, Pat Hogan has now produced a companion list covering numismatic books on the Far East. All books are currently available from stock in the U.S.A. and, where sterling prices are shown, also in the U.K. If you have difficulty in obtaining them from your usual sources, ONS may be able to help you. Please let me know of any errors or omissions.
